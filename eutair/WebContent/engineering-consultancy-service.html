Engineering Consultancy

Service Description

We provide our customers with insight of various industrial sectors and offer guidance on setting up of the utilities based on available case studies/data. These designing case studies are based completely on actual running data gathered and recorded over the years by our team lead by our Principal Consultant Dr. Shweta Gupta (Doctorate in Chemical Engineering)

Our consulting engineers are independent of any association with suppliers/ vendors, fabricators/ erectors and contractors so as to ensure that their services are unbiased.
