Compressed Air Experts

Eutair Equipments with a strong cumulative experience of 30 years is based at New Delhi, India.

Eutair Equipments is a leading Engineering Company, engaged in the engineering, sales and services of capital equipments to various Industrial sectors. The Industrial segments we cover are Injection Moulding, Packaging, Fabrication, Food & Beverages, Electronics, Marine, Cement, Steel, Power, Petrochemicals, Oil & Gas, Paper, Sugar, Distillery, Glass etc. We have executed umpteen numbers of projects for our prestigious customers.

Our Team offers high quality products and services, backed by adequate Infrastructure with well-defined systems and procedures, dedicated and talented workforce comprising of Engineers, Service Personnel at different levels, committed to deliver best to their capability..

Our Range of customers include Progress Rail – A Caterpillar Company, Global Sugarcane, Boyd Corporation, Delta Autocorp, Titagarh Wagon Ltd, Vahdam Teas, Bircher Cellpack, Yash Glass, Chempharm, Hydrogen Gentech etc..

Eutair Equipments is associated with renowned capital manufacturing companies offering industrial equipment from Concept to Commissioning on EP, EPC, turnkey basis as per customer need..

Our projects include design, supply, erect and commissioning of complete Compressed Air System along with heat exchangers, cooling towers, plant air & water piping system. We design, develop and charge up all the three sides- Generation, Distribution & Demand. We have executed projects in India and abroad. We also undertake after sales services for these projects. Execution of projects in Nigeria, Nepal, Bangladesh & Myanmar has brought us on to global map.
