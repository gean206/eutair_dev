Service Description

Be it a simple supply of equipment or be it complete EPC turnkey solution with an integration of design, engineering, project management, procurement and execution of civil work, piping, electrical, remote monitoring & controls, we do it all for your peace of mind.

We undertake EPC Turnkey projects that includes design, supply, erect and commission complete Compressed Air System along with heat exchangers, cooling towers, plant air & water piping system. We design, develop and charge up all the three sides- Generation, Distribution & Demand. We have executed projects in India and abroad. We also undertake after sales services for these projects.
