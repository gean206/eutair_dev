Service Description

Our core strength lies in designing, packaging and executing non-standard/ customized medium and high pressure air requirements in the most economical manner and we believe in passing the benefit on to our esteemed customers. We incorporate add-ons and do packaging for bare compressors with desired makes of piston type reciprocating air compressors as per the exact requirements in the most economical manner that not only makes your system efficient but also reduce high cost burden on your annual budgets on new procurement and maintenance. We are very competitive on price and lead time.

Our clients have greatly benefited from customized air compressors. Customized air compressors vary in price but can be made in a way that best suits the facility it will be used for.

Facts about customized air compressors:

  • There are a variety of air compressor types that can be used for useful purposes
  • Air compressors can be powered by electricity, gas, or diesel
  • Customized air compressors can be purchased new or used
  • There are rotary screw, rotary centrifugal and reciprocating air compressors
  • Air compressor package can be skid mounted
  • Desired motor make can be incorporated
