Compressed Air Experts

Eutair is a team of enthusiastic professionals with a strong cumulative experience of 30 years. We have over the years accepted industry challenges, and these very challenges made us what we are today “Compressed Air Experts”.

Eutair is a leading Engineering Company, engaged in the engineering, sales and services of capital equipments to various Industrial sectors. The Industrial segments we cover are Moulding, Packaging, Fabrication, Food & Beverages, Electronics, Marine, Cement, Steel, Power, Petrochemicals, Oil & Gas, Paper, Sugar, Distillery, Glass etc. We have executed umpteen numbers of projects for our prestigious customers.

Eutair list of customers includes Progress Rail – A Caterpillar Company, Global Sugarcane, Boyd Corporation, Delta Autocorp, Titagarh Wagon Ltd, Vahdam Teas, Bircher Cellpack, Yash Glass, Chempharm, Hydrogen Gentech etc..

Eutair is associated with renowned capital manufacturing companies offering industrial equipment from Concept to Commissioning on EP, EPC, turnkey basis as per customer needs..

Eutair projects include design, supply, erect and commissioning of complete Compressed Air System along with heat exchangers, cooling towers, plant air & water piping system. We design, develop and charge up all the three sides- Generation, Distribution & Demand. We have executed projects in India and abroad. We also undertake after sales services for these projects. Execution of projects in Nigeria, Nepal, Bangladesh & Myanmar has brought us on to global map.
